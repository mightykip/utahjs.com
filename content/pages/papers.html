We are pleased to announce that the UtahJS JavaScript Conference, part of Silicon Slopes Summit, is requesting paper submissions for talks. This is going to be big. Think of hundreds of people, all in the same place, learning about JavaScript and HTML5!

If you have a topic you would like to speak on at our conference, please do not hesitate to propose a talk.

Key dates

Call for Papers Open Now
Call for Papers Closes December 31, 2011
Proposal Acceptance January 15, 2012
Main Conference March 29-31, 2012

Speaker Benefits

We will pay your way here (airline ticket), your motel and give you a free pass to the event!

Presentation Format

Each speaker will have 45 minutes + 5 minutes for questions.
